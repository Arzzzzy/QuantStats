Probability and Methods of Counting

Formula

Permutations:

Permutation of n objects taken r at a time (without repetition): P(n, r) = n! / (n - r)!

Permutation of n objects taken r at a time (with repetition): P(n, r) = n^r

Combinations:

Combination of n objects taken r at a time (without repetition): C(n, r) = n! / (r! (n - r)!)

Combination of n objects taken r at a time (with repetition): C(n+r-1, r) = (n+r-1)! / (r! (n - 1)!)
